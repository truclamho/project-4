Mai Mai In Your Heart: Vietnamese Folklore

Spring 2025

Embark on an exciting journey through Vietnam’s rich folklore and cultural heritage, reimagined through the lens of visual storytelling and marketing techniques. Stories passed down through generations are taken and designed to resonate with global audiences through interactive packaging and multimedia visuals. The project breathes life into these stories through typographic illustrations, a creative video, and an intuitive website. Each element is infused with emotion and symbolism, shaped by my artistic style and cultural connection. By fusing ancient mythology with contemporary design, Mãi Mãi offers a captivating cultural experience that celebrates and preserves Vietnam’s history while merging tradition with innovative approaches to shaping modern culture and marketing. It is the culmination of everything I’ve learned over my years of study, blended with the essence of my culture, and reflects a piece of my identity and journey.
